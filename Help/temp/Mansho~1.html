Form :- OrderEntry /I
 
After Order Entry is created ,on clicking Save Order Entry button , Order Entry is saved and form Order Details/I is selected where Order Details can be entered . 
You can select Order template , by clicking Order Form Template button . On clicking the Billing Address and Shipping address is assigned to the Order Entry Form . 
You get information about Order form on clicking Order Form information button .
You get help about Order Form on clicking Order Form Help button .

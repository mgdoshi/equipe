Form :- ItemGroup / I
 
Once new ItemClass is created , on clicking Save ItemClass button , form ItemGroup /T is selected where new ItemClass is reflected .
If you want to save the existing ItemClass and create new ItemClass , click Save ItemClass And Create New ItemClass button. On clicking ItemClass is saved and form ItemClass/I is selected where again new ItemClass can be created .
You get information about ItemGroup form on clicking ItemGroup Form Information button.
You get help about ItemGroup Form on clicking ItemGroup Form Help button .

Form :- ItemClass / T
 
To create new ItemClass click Create NewItem Class button . On clicking this button form ItemClass /I is selected where new ItemClass can be created .
Click the check box in the table to delete the items corresponding to it.
You get information about ItemClass form on clicking ItemClass Form Information button.
You get help about ItemClass Form on clicking ItemClass Form Help button .

Form :- ItemClass / I
 
Once new ItemClass is created , on clicking Save ItemClass button , form ItemClass /T is selected where new ItemClass is reflected .
If you want to save the existing ItemClass and create new ItemClass , click Save ItemClass And Create New ItemClass button. On clicking ItemClass is saved and form ItemClass/I is selected where again new ItemClass can be created .
You get information about ItemClass form on clicking ItemClass Form Information button.
You get help about ItemClass Form on clicking ItemClass Form Help button .
